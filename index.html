company MISSION TO TECHNOLOGY & CYBERSECURITY

Company is committed to protecting the security of its IT/OT/IoT assets in accordance with industry standards, good cybersecurity practices and applicable regulatory requirements. To protect against an ever-evolving threat landscape and to ensure Company is able to meet its business objectives, it is essential that cyber risks are reduced to an acceptable level. In this regard, the Cybersecurity Department offers its guidance and extends support for effectively managing cyber risks within Company environment and with the partners Company engages with.

Company will be fuelled by a secure-by-design concept that embeds a risk mindset from the onset of all technology-based and cognitive solution projects, with proactive security monitoring and state-of-the-art threat intelligence that enables a secure digital utopia.
